About me.

I’m Sara, Art Enthusiast and a future Blockchain Developer, currently studying
Blockchain Development at Medieinstitutet.
As an artist and painter, I am particularly drawn to the intersection of
blockchain technology and art. My previous experience in web development
and graphic design has allowed me to develop a keen eye for detail and a
strong understanding of user experience design principles. I believe that
these skills will be beneficial in the development of user-friendly and
visually appealing blockchain applications.

Before I started studying Blockchain development, I was working a few years
as a Service Manager at campsites, Site Manager at a gym chain and Staffing
Manager at a cleaning company. During these experiences, I have grown both
as a leader and as a team player. I have a strong sense of responsibility
for my work, while my coworkers are also able to place high amount of trust
in my ability. I have also gained high tolerance towards stressful situations
and the ability to deliver under pressure. I work well independently and I
tend to be proactive when the situation demands so.

During 2020-2022, I studied art, deep diving into the aspects of my own artistry
as a painter. Living in Spain for one year during this time, I had a quality time
to reflect on the work processes around painting. Over the past few years, I have
come to realize that I thrive when I get to work with something practical and
creative. I am highly capable of performing the administrative work but it is
the sense of creativity that I enjoy the most with any task. In my spare time I
find an outlet for this creativity through my painting, drawing, music, as well as
cooking and baking. Other interests are exercise, nature and hiking.
